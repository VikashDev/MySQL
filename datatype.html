Data Types > INT or INTEGER It holds whole number b/w -32,768 & 32,767 either it is negative or positive. Syntax :- roll int(11); > DEC or DECIMAL(size, p) Holds fixed point numbers. Size is the total number of digits & p is the number of digits after the decimal points. Syntax :- column_name dec(size,p); Eg:- price dec(4,2);
